Our services

For individuals

  • Legal advice: oral, written
  • Legal implications
  • Site audit and consultation by the advocate for the provision of legal assistance in criminal, administrative, civil, commercial cases
  • Negotiation, preparation of letters / claims
  • Preparation of lawyer's requests
  • Mediation
    • Representation in Commercial Courts: cases in disputes arising from the conclusion, modification, termination and execution of economic contracts; bankruptcy cases; affairs arising from corporate relations; cases in disputes concerning the rights of securities, intellectual property protection
    • Representation in the courts of General jurisdiction: family, civil, residential, land, labor, banking, inheritance, intellectual affairs, as well as cases arising out of traffic accidents, appeal of fines
    • Representation in Administrative Courts: tax disputes; pension disputes; disputes with the subject of authority regarding the appeal of its decisions; disputes regarding the admission of citizens to the public service, its passage, dismissal; disputes arising out of the conclusion, execution, termination, cancellation or invalidation of invalid administrative agreements; cases concerning the appeal of decisions of the attestation, competition, medical and social expert commissions; disputes with the manager of public information; administrative disputes over decisions, actions or inactivity of the subjects of power authorities; cases related to the stay of foreigners and stateless persons in the territory of Ukraine; cases concerning decisions, actions or inactivity of the state executive service
    • Representation in the International Commercial Arbitration Court at the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Ukraine
    • Representation in the Arbitration Courts
    • Protecting the client's interests as a lawyer in criminal cases: in the police, prosecutor's office, SSU, NABU. Protection of the interests of the accused / victim / witness during the investigation; assistance during interrogation, eye rates, investigative experiments; visit of the accused in the detention center; appeal of the chosen measure of preventive measure; preparation of petitions; collection of materials characterizing the accused person; protection of interests in court
    • Supporting enforcement proceedings
    • Legal preparation and analysis of agreements, contracts
    • Legal support of agreements on the sale and purchase of movable and immovable assets
    • Registration, creation of associations of co-owners of multi-apartment buildings (ACMHs)
    • Registration of LLC, PP, Individual enterpreneurship, preparation of all constitutional documents
    • Registration of ownership through NCAA, notarial offices, recognition of property rights through court
    • Supports tax inspections, cancellation of tax decisions and notices of the DPI in court and extrajudicial order
    • Legal services for foreigners: obtaining a work permit; temporary residence permit; permanent residence permit; taxpayer's account card; registration of enterprises with foreign investments; solving the issue of deportation (creation); divorce by foreigners of a marriage registered in Ukraine
    • The list of legal services is not exhaustive, it depends on the specific needs of the client
  • Subscriber service - OUTSOURCING:
  • Legal advice on providing suggestions on options for solving civil, economic, corporate, labor, tax, land, customs, administrative, criminal law issues
  • Submission of lawyer's requests (collection of documents for a court)
  • Preparation, analysis, correction of civil, commercial contracts and agreements
  • Legal analysis of the enterprise's activity (legal audit)
    • Legal provision of labor relations (the development of contracts, labor contracts, collective agreements, treaties on material liability)
    • Participation of the lawyer of Profi Lawyer JSC in carrying out inspections by the tax authorities, state control bodies and law enforcement agencies, including searches and compulsory penetration into the premises
    • Representation of client's interests in various state structures, prosecutor's office, police, NABU
    • Representation of client interests in courts of all jurisdictions
    • Preparation of statements of claim, applications for the guarantee of a claim, petitions, explanations, references, objections, appeals and cassation complaints
    • Support of enforcement proceedings
